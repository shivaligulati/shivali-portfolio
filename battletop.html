Exploring Engineering



About


For our academy competition, we were required to CAD battle tops which could spin the longest among our classmates. My top won 3rd place for spinning for the longest period of time among 120 entries in this contest. From this project, I learned how to use the revolve command which allowed my top to have a sharp tip in comparrison to other caded tops. In addition to revolve, I used the mirror, arc, and smooth commands to create the shape of my 3D top.
